Biography

Professionally trained in Dharma Yoga (Hatha Raja Yoga) in Guadalajara (2017). Mars has had an immersion in various styles of movement: Polynesian dances (since 1999), contemporary dance, African dance. She has taken dance classes and courses in countries such as Ecuador, Senegal, Germany. As well as in CDMX, Veracruz, Oaxaca and Baja California. Fourteen years ago she began her relationship with the Toltecayotl on the red path, through the Temazcal, dance to the Earth, dance to the Moon, where she found a philosophy of life that reconnects with her pre-Hispanic roots, healing and the development of consciousness. Astrology has been her passion for more than a decade, receiving her training in Evolutionary Astrology at the Astrotherapy School of Pablo Flores Laymus. She is currently a yoga teacher, astrotherapist, women's circle guide, facilitator of sound baths, meditation circles with cacao and dance, union ceremonies and spiritual retreats sharing in Germany, California and Mexico.
